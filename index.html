Computational Dialogues

Computational Dialogues (CODI) is a course that introduces live coding as an art practice for engaging in dialogues. Live coding practice teaches and develops improvisational skills. Through weekly exercises, students are taught esoteric programming languages to create audiovisual and performative works.
